Dal

What is Dal?

Dal, specifically parippu, is a dish from kerala based on lentils and ghee.

It is a staple among malayalees.

Ingredients you will need:

  • 8 oz. red lentils
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 onions
  • 4 curry leaves
  • ¼ teaspoon saffron powder
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ lime
  • 1 green hot pepper
  • Salt

The method:

  1. Wash the lentils well by changing the water at least three times
  2. Boil them in the water with the saffron, chili powder, turmeric, curry leaves and cloves.
  3. After about 15 minutes, when the color of the lentils changes from red to yellow, remove from the heat and pour into a large bowl.
  4. Heat the oil in a very large skillet over medium heat. Add the mustard, fenugreek, and cumin seeds and fry for 1 minute
  5. Add the onion and garlic and mix well for 20 seconds
  6. Add the boiled lentils and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes
  7. Remove from heat and add the lime juice
  8. Add the raw or fried green hot pepper on top (optional).
  9. Serve hot with rice or bread.
