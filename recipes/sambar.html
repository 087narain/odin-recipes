What is sambar?

One of the finest dishes South India has to offer.

It is a vegetable stew of sorts, rife with flavours and an assortment of vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, okras, tomatoes and lentils. It is one of the most important dishes to try.

Ingredients

  • Masala powder
  • Water
  • Okra
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Lentils
  • Curry leaves
  • Vegetable stock
  • Sambar powder

How to cook Sambar

  1. Pressure cook lentils until they become soft and mushy.
  2. Cook the vegetables together in a mixture under boiling water until tender.
  3. Mix the lentils under vegetable stock with sambar powder. Simmer them together.
  4. Add the dal and simmer these alognside the lentil/vegetable stock mix.
  5. Fry the spices in ghee/oil and pour them over the cooked sambar.
  6. Your sambar is now ready! Serve with dosa or idli.
